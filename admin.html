Creating a Payment Link

1. Log in to your Stripe Dashboard.
2. Go to Payment Links → Create a new payment link.
3. Add the product, price, and any options (e.g. shipping).
4. Click Create Link, then copy the generated URL.
5. Paste that URL into the product's "Stripe Payment Link" field in the Products tab.
